How to Connect Your Chat App

1

Start Pico AI Server

Launch Pico AI Server on your Mac and ensure it's running in the background.

2

Configure Your Chat App

In your chat app settings, set the API endpoint to: http://localhost:11434

3

Select Your Model

Choose from 300+ available models in Pico AI Server, including DeepSeek R1, Llama, and Gemma.

4

Start Chatting

Your chat app will now use Pico AI Server for all AI interactions, keeping everything private and local.
